Hi, I'm Tristan! I am a Belgian graphic designer who has an infuriating tendency to draw on every little piece of paper I can find.

Here's why I created this website:

Popular social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok and Facebook offer many advantages for sharing one's creative work. Lately however, I realized the companies behind these platforms have become less interested in building a community of creative people and more interested in keeping people hooked to maximize profits. The stifling of my creativity in exchange for ads and repetitive content didn't sit right with me. I therefore decided that I needed my own space for sharing my art with the world, and built this little website as a result.
